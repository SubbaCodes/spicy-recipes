Spicy Turkey Chilli

Description

This a very hearty and spicy chili, one I believe all will love! Serve topped with Cheddar cheese.

Ingredients

  • turkey meat
  • kidney beans
  • stewed tomatoes
  • chili seasoning mix
  • green chile peppers
  • tomato sauce
  • onions

Steps

  1. Step 1
    In a slow cooker, combine turkey, beans, tomatoes, chili seasoning, chile peppers, tomato sauce, onion and water.
  2. Step 2
    Cook on low 3 to 4 hours.
  3. Step 2
    Simmer 15minutes and serve hot with bread or rice.
